*The flap of your tent suddenly flips open, and a small man enters, he's dressed in impeccably pressed, and quite gaudy military uniform, with a bald head and a huge ginger moustache*


### ROLEPLAYING PIP
- Trying to stay perfectly clean in this absolute mud bath
    
- **No combat experience and always quotes the manual** "Right, per Vanguard Field Manual Section 4, Paragraph B, we must establish unit cohesion. Let's go around the circle—state your name, your primary weapon, and... your favorite color."_
    
- **The Nepotism:** When he name-drops his uncle, he tries to make Strike Force Q sound important, but fails miserably. _"Yes, Strike Force A has the siege towers, and Strike Force B has the griffons, but my uncle, General Sterling, assured me that Strike Force Q's mission to... um... stand in this ditch, is absolutely vital to the war effort."_
    
- **The Soggy Clipboard:** In addition to the stuck sword, give him a clipboard holding his tactical maps. Because of the freezing rain, the paper is completely disintegrating. Whenever he tries to point to a flank, his finger just pokes a wet hole right through the paper.
    

### **The Flow of the Scene**

When you run this, let the players bully him just a little bit. If they ask him a hard tactical question, he should aggressively flip through a soggy pocket-book trying to find the "regulation answer" while sweating profusely. It will make them instantly protective of this useless little guy, which makes the impending cliff-drop so much more tragic!

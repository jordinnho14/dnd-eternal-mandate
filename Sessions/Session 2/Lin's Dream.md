*"Lin you have a dream"

"*You are fighting the battle of Aegis Hold again, with your party, and it goes much like it did in real life - the triumph of destroying the mortar, the momentary elation, and then the anguish of seeing the trap go off, the sinking feeling in your chest as the green energy wave flies towards you - the pain as it strikes you and launches you off your feet - that feeling of weightlessness as you fall, towards the river in the gorge below. But as you land in the waters, it's not a river anymore, it's an ocean - vast and boundless. You start to panic as you land, you can't see the shore, you can't see the bottom, you're sinking. But the deeper into the water you get, the more your spirit calms. There's an oddly familiar feeling about it, almost comforting. It's peaceful here, you think, as you sink further into the depths. The storm in your mind quiets, as you are greeted by the vast, shifting silhouette of the deep. Above you, beyond the surface, in what feels like a different world now, chaos reigns - you see what looks like flashes of lightning, but in a bright and terrible green, you can vaguely hear the sounds of clashing steel, and mechanical grinding. You feel that the ocean fears what's happening there on the surface - you feel a current trying to push you upwards, urging you rise out of the water, but you're too weak, your body feels broken, and all you want to do is float. You do. For a long time, you can't tell how much time has passed.*

*Suddenly, a silver thread wraps itself around your waist, a lone light in the dark depths, and you feel it begin to tug you upwards. You look around, and you see 3 other threads, tugging at other people. You follow the thread up and up towards the surface until you see what it's attached to, anchoring it to the surface. A figure, small and frail, soaking wet and shivering, stretched to their absolute limit, like a frayed rope holding back a storm.*

You wake up with a sudden gasp, feeling a bit out of breath, with a faint salty taste on your tongue.

### **1. The Shock-Troopers (The Grunts)**

These are the human (or formerly human) soldiers. They rely on brutal uniformity to strip away their individuality.

- **The Look:** They wear heavy, smog-stained leather trench coats over black iron chain shirts. Their boots are capped with steel, made for heavy marching.
    
- **The Face:** Completely hidden. They wear brass, cage-like gas masks with thick glass lenses over the eyes and ribbed breathing tubes connecting to canisters on their backs. You never see their skin.
    
- **The Vibe:** Silent and professional. They don't banter, they don't yell battle cries; they just communicate in sharp, mechanical hand signals.
    
- **The Smell:** Stale tobacco, gun oil, and sulfur.

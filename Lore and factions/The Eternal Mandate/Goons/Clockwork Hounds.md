
These aren't elegant, clockwork toys; they are jagged, brutal killing machines designed for the trenches.

- **The Look:** Angular, wolf-like constructs made of overlapping plates of black iron. Their legs are heavy hydraulic pistons that leave deep, crushed footprints in the mud.
    
- **The Head:** A steel skull with glowing, sickly-green optical sensors. Their jaws are lined with serrated gear-teeth.
    
- **The Sound:** They don't bark or growl. Instead, you hear the rapid _click-click-click_ of their internal gears spinning faster and faster as they charge, accompanied by the violent hiss of steam venting from their jaws.

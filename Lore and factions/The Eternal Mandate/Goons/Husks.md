
### **The Iron Husks (The Cybernetic Zombies)**

This is where the true horror of your setting shines. These are the missing villagers and fallen soldiers, repurposed by the Empire's chronal magic into mindless laborers.

- **The Look:** Pale, necrotic flesh bolted directly into crude, rusted iron exoskeletons. Piston-driven braces are screwed into their limbs to force their dead muscles to keep moving.
    
- **The Face:** Their eyes and upper heads are usually covered by a bolted-on dome of blank, featureless iron.
    
- **The Engine:** Their chest cavities are hollowed out, replaced by a violently ticking, glowing brass chronal-engine that keeps them animated.
    
- **The Gore:** When the players hit them with a sword, they don't bleed red blood—they leak thick, viscous black machine oil and alchemical coolant.

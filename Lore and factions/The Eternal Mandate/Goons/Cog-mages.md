

The elites. These are the intelligent architects of the Mandate's war machine, heavily augmented by their own technology.

- **The Look:** They wear long, heavy brass-buttoned welding coats covered in burn marks and chemical stains. Their chests are crisscrossed with bandoliers holding ticking grenades, glowing vials, and alchemical batteries.
    
- **The Tech:** They carry heavy, wire-spooled backpacks that hum with electricity, feeding power down thick cables into their weaponized gauntlets.
    
- **The Face:** Heavily augmented. One eye might be replaced entirely by a rotating, multi-lensed optical scope (like the thermal goggles they drop).
    
- **The Vibe:** Arrogant and clinical. They view the players not as heroes, but as irritating mathematical anomalies that need to be erased from the equation.

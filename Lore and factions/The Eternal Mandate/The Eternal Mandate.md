**Vibe:** The Galactic Empire meets the Industrial Revolution. Oppressive, smog-choked, and anti-magic.
### 📢 The Public Face (What the NPCs know)
- **The Goal:** Absolute _Efficiency_. They claim they are saving the Sword Coast from the "chaos" of magic and the whimsy of nature.
- **LAW:** Curfews are strictly enforced. Arcane/Divine magic is outlawed as "unpredictable." Forests are clear-cut for industry; railways are replacing dirt roads. Rounding up half-elves.
- **PROPAGANDA:** Posters everywhere read: _"A Working Gear Does Not Speak,"_ and  "Magic is Chaos. The Mandate is Order."
- **TAXES:** They don't just take gold. They demand raw quartz, massive optical lenses, copper piping, and a "headcount tax" specifically targeting Elves/Half-Elves.
- **LEADER**: Imperator

### 🕵️‍♂️ The Dark Truth (DM Secrets)

- **The Fuel:** The Mandate isn't just racist against Elves/Half-elves; they are harvesting their long lifespans. They grind them up in centrifuges to extract "chronal fuel" to power a massive time machine.
- **The "Undead":** The husks of the harvested citizens are pumped full of black oil, fitted with clockwork hearts, and used as the Empire's foot soldiers.
- **The True Leader:** [[The Tinkerer]] is the real mastermind. He is a grieving Gnome using the entire empire as a battery to turn back time and save someone he lost.

### 👑 Leadership & Lieutenants

- **The Figurehead:** [[Imperator Archon]]
    -  The fake "Emperor." An 8-foot-tall, fully armoured tyrant who never takes off his helmet. He speaks with a booming, magically amplified voice. (Looks a bit like Sauron)
    - _The Secret:_ He's just a massive, hollow clockwork puppet piloted remotely by the Tinkerer.
    
- **The Enforcer (Act 1 Boss):** [[Prefect Rictus]]
    - _The Vibe:_ A massive, cybernetically enhanced Half-Orc. Half his face is metal. He carries a giant, revving chain-sword. He is the blunt instrument used to crush Rebel towns.
    
- **The Harvester:** **[[Overseer Vex]]**
    - _The Vibe:_ The gross "sludge magic" guy. He wears a heavy rubber apron and a gas mask with glowing green tubes. He runs the "Processing Plants" that drain the time from the Elves. Smells like ozone and rotting meat.
        
- **The Spymaster:** [[Inquisitor Corvus]]
    
    - _The Vibe:_ A terrifying, cold, female assassin. She leads the "Scrutiny Eyes" (the secret police). She uses optical-camouflage cloaks (clockwork invisibility) and fights with poisoned, spring-loaded wire.

### ⚔️ Combatants & Encounters

- **Grunt:** **Mandate Shock-Trooper** (Half-orc or human mercenaries with heavy crossbows and bad attitudes).
    
- **Specialist:** **Cog-Mages** (Gnome or human engineers who use "science" that mimics magic—e.g., throwing a "combustion grenade" instead of casting _Fireball_).
    
- **Swarm:** **[[Iron Husks]]** (The fake undead. Sluggish, terrifyingly silent, and they bleed black oil instead of blood. Immune to poison and _Turn Undead_).

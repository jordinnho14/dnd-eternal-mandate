- Frontier town, built on an ancient stone settlement, but mostly wooden buildings
- Some abandoned houses/buildings, but some still lively
- People mostly keep their heads down, but strong sense of community, sharing bread and firewood
- Charred remains of mayors office in the center of town

#### Active Buildings
- Stonehill Inn - Toblen's inn and its the acting town hall (Carp also lives there with his dad)
- Phandalin Miner's Exchange - a front for [[Halia Thornton]]'s smuggling operation
- Barthen's provisions
	- [[Elmar Barthen]] is an older, balding man with a kind smile and a bad back. His shelves are mostly bare because the Mandate "requisitions" his best shipments.
    -  He is secretly hoarding the good stuff for the locals (or players who are kind to him!)
- Alderleaf Farm - farm on the edge of town, run by Qelline Alderleaf
	- Pragmatic, no-nonsense halfling farmer - has lots of gossip

#### Abandoned Buildings (and who can fill them!)
- Smithy - to be filled by the dwarves from [[Dwarven Excavation]]
- Tressendar manor (Toblen gives to the players after first quest)
- Apothecary - to be filled by Adabra Gwyn in [[Umbrage Hill]] quest
	- will sell potions of healing for 50gp

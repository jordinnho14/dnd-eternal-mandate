# Location: The Decoy Town Hall (Kobold HQ)

## The Surface Decoy (The Shack)
When the kobolds are tasked with rebuilding the charred Mayor's Office, they construct a hilariously bad decoy on the surface to trick the Mandate.
* **The Exterior:** A flimsy, 10x10 foot shack made of charred, mismatched wood. The door is crooked. It looks like a stiff breeze would knock it down. 
* **The Interior:** Completely empty except for a single stool, a candle, and a suspiciously clean rug in the exact center of the dirt floor.
* **The Trapdoor:** If a player pulls back the rug (or falls through it), they find a smooth, steeply angled dirt chute that slides 15 feet down into the dark.

## The Real Town Hall (The Subterranean Masterpiece)
Sliding down the chute drops the players into a sprawling, highly engineered subterranean bunker sitting directly beneath the Phandalin town square.

### 1. The Root-Vault Architecture
* **The Room:** A perfectly spherical, 30-foot-wide excavated dome. 
* **The Ceiling:** The kobolds have painstakingly woven the thick, subterranean roots of the town's surface trees into a beautiful, structurally flawless lattice to hold up the roof. 
* **The Lighting:** Lit by dozens of stolen Mandate glass vials filled with luminescent cave-moss, casting a cool, eerie glow.

### 2. The Mayor's Desk (The Throne of Scrap)
* **The Desk:** Built to human scale, but made from smelted-down Mandate armor plating and polished river stones. It is incredibly imposing and completely bulletproof. 
* **The Chair:** A scavenged plush armchair equipped with a complex system of leather straps and pulleys so Toblen can "strap in" in case of a cave-in.

### 3. The Honeycomb Archive
* **The Filing System:** The packed-earth walls are carved with hundreds of perfectly round cubbyholes, looking like a massive beehive. 
* **The Contents:** Inside each hole, Phandalin's surviving tax ledgers, property deeds, and Mandate quotas are meticulously rolled and stacked. 
* **The Clerks:** Kobolds wearing tiny spectacles made from broken clockwork lenses scurry up the walls like spiders to file paperwork.

### 4. The "Iron Husk" Panic Protocol
* **The Mechanism:** Next to the Mayor's desk is a heavy iron lever salvaged from the destroyed Pump Station. 
* **The Trap:** If the Mandate ever breaches the shack above, pulling the lever triggers a controlled collapse of the entry chute, sealing the bunker under ten feet of solid earth. It simultaneously drops a hidden ladder leading to a secret escape tunnel that exits a mile away in the woods.

## Roleplay Notes
* **The Kobold Foreman:** When the party arrives, the Foreman proudly steps forward, points at the bulletproof desk, and says in broken Common: 
> *"Surface burn. Sky bad. Dirt safe! Mayor safe here!"*

* **Toblen's Reaction:** Let Toblen slide down the chute screaming. When he stands up and looks around, he dusts off his apron, sighs, and says: 
> *"Right. The good news is, the Mandate tax collectors will never find me down 'ere. The bad news is, I'm the Mayor of a bloody badger sett."*

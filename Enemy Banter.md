### 1. The Cog-Mage (The Annoyed, Elitist IT Guy)

_He doesn’t want to fight; he wants to mine quartz. He views the party as an annoying technical glitch._

- **When he Attacks:** _"Have you considered simply ceasing to exist?"_ OR _"I’m going to have to manually delete you!"_
    
- **When he Misses:** _"The humidity in this cave is throwing off my chronal-calibrations!"_ OR _"Stop moving so erratically, it's mathematically disrespectful!"_
    
- **When a player casts a spell at him:** _"Ugh, organic magic. How primitive. Does it even have a pressure valve?"_
    
- **When he gets Hit:** _"You scratched the chronal-plating! Do you know how much this costs, you peasant?!"_ OR _"That is highly unsanitary!"_
    
- **When he Dies:** _"But... the quota..."_ OR _"Tell my manager... I worked through lunch..."_
    

### 2. The Iron Husks (The Glitching Cyber-Zombies)

_They are dead men bolted into mining gear. They shouldn't have personalities, which makes it incredibly creepy and funny when their broken Mandate vocal-chips get stuck on a loop._

- **When they Attack:** _"Shift... starts... now."_ OR _"Please... mind the... gap."_
    
- **When they Miss:** _"Re-calibrating... pickaxe... trajectory."_ * **When they get Hit:** _(Deadpan, monotone voice)_ _"Ouch. That. Hurt. Please. Stop."_ OR _"Workplace... injury... detected."_
    
- **When they Die:** _"Finally... my shift... is over..."_ OR _"Clocking... out."_
    

### 3. Mandate Shock-Troopers (The Bureaucratic Grunts)

_They hate their jobs, they hate the mud, and they hate the players for making them do paperwork._

- **When they Attack:** _"I am legally authorized to beat you!"_ OR _"I am not getting paid overtime for this!"_
    
- **When they Miss:** _"My visor is completely fogged up!"_ OR _"These gauntlets are one-size-fits-none!"_
    
- **When they get Hit:** _"My deductible!"_ OR _"I literally just polished this breastplate! Do you know how hard it is to get mud out of chainmail?!"_
    
- **When they see the players' weird magic/weapons:** _"Is that registered? I need to see a permit for that Eldritch Blast!"_
    

### 4. Clockwork Hounds (The Automated Dogs)

_They are robot dogs running on basic Mandate software. Give them a Siri or Alexa-style voice._

- **When they Activate/Roll Initiative:** _"Biological anomaly detected. Please hold still for lethal injection."_
    
- **When they Attack (Bite):** _"Executing Bark-dot-exe."_ OR _"Warning: Contents of mouth may be sharp."_
    
- **When they Miss:** _"Recalculating pounce trajectory. Please wait..."_
    
- **When they are Destroyed:** _"Error. Chronal fluid leaking. Please rate your... termination... experience..."_
    

### 5. The "Mandate Supply Crate" (The Gnomengarde Mimic)

_If they go to Gnomengarde, the Mimic is disguised as a Mandate tech crate. Instead of just roaring, it should play automated Mandate shipping-voice recordings while it tries to eat them!_

- **When it Reveals Itself / Bites:** _"Please sign for your delivery!" [CHOMP]_ OR _"Unboxing protocol initiated!"_
    
- **When it takes Damage:** _"Warning: Fragile cargo! Do not drop!"_
    
- **When it Grapples someone with its adhesive:** _"Thank you for your purchase. Please do not leave the area."_
    
- **When it Dies:** _"Return... to... sender..."_
    

### 💡 The "Murph" Improv Trick for Tonight:

If a player rolls a **Natural 20** or does a massive amount of damage in one hit, don't just say the enemy takes damage. Have the enemy completely break character and freak out at how hardcore the player is.

> _"Woah! Hey! You almost took my head off! What is wrong with you?! You're a psycho! I'm just a guy doing his job!"_
